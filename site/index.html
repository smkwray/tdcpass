Treasury Contribution to Deposits

TDC effects on deposits and independently measured non-TDC deposits.

TDC Pass studies how Treasury contributions affect deposits, and what independent evidence says about non-Treasury deposit creation. That independent evidence is concentrated in lending and excludes treasury other-checkable and rest-of-world deposit channels under current evidence.

Also excluded

Trade, QE, offshore routing

  • Exports and imports are theoretically relevant but not same-scope deposit-settlement measures.
  • Fed MBS / QE incidence is real in theory but not promotable under current seller-incidence limits.
  • Offshore / correspondent / foreign-bank routing remains context and leakage, not a strict creator channel.
